About the AI Health Risk Assessment

AI Health Risk Assessment is an innovative web application that utilizes artificial intelligence to analyze symptoms and medical history, providing users with early insights into potential health conditions.

Our Mission

We aim to democratize health analysis by offering accessible, intelligent, and secure tools to aid in early health risk detection and management.

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Backend: Python with Flask
  • AI Engine: Gemini API integrated with ML models trained on health datasets
